Who We Are

Founded in Jaipur, the gem capital of India, Zee Rodium has established itself as a pioneer in modern jewelry manufacturing. We specialize in electroformed jewelry, crafting exquisite gemstone rings, developing innovative gold-plated connectors, and pioneering conductive ink technologies. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us a trusted name in the jewelry industry.

Meet the Founder

Nitin Katta

Founder & CEO

With over two decades of experience in jewelry manufacturing, Nitin Katta has revolutionized the industry through innovative techniques and unwavering commitment to quality. His vision of combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology has established Zee Rodium as a leader in the jewelry manufacturing sector.

Under his leadership, Zee Rodium has pioneered several breakthrough technologies in electroformed jewelry and conductive ink applications, setting new standards in the industry.
